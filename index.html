What is Plant Monitoring System ?

A plant monitoring system employs sensors and data analysis to oversee key conditions like soil moisture, light exposure, temperature, and nutrient levels. By gathering real-time data, it enables precise management of plant health and growth. This technology helps prevent issues like over-watering, ensures optimal environmental conditions, and fosters healthier and more productive plants in various settings, from homes to agricultural fields.
